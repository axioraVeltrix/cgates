Our process

Steps to Building
Your College Roadmap

At College Gates, we guide students step by step — from discovering their goals to preparing for exams, building applications, and confidently moving toward the right university.

1
🎯

Understand your goals

We begin by understanding your academic interests, future major, dream universities, and the direction you want your future to take.

2
📊

Assess your profile

We review your grades, extracurriculars, achievements, English level, and overall profile to identify strengths and what should be improved.

3
🏫

Choose the right universities

We help you build a balanced university list based on your goals, major, academic background, budget, scholarships, and admission chances.

4
📝

Prepare for tests and applications

We support you with SAT, TOEFL, IELTS, essays, activity lists, personal statements, application strategy, and important deadlines.

5
🚀

Start your next chapter

After submitting applications, we help you compare offers, scholarships, final choices, and confidently prepare for your transition to university life.
